Track inventory with continuous, periodic, and hybrid models for optimal control

Continuous Review (Q-System)

Steps: Monitor stock levels continuously; reorder a fixed quantity Q when inventory drops to reorder point R.

Use Case: Ideal when real-time data is available and quick replenishment is needed.

Example: An online grocery service restocks milk automatically when levels fall below 50 packs.

Periodic Review (P-System)

Steps: Review inventory at regular intervals P and order up to a predefined target level T.

Use Case: Works well when placing grouped or scheduled orders with suppliers.

Example: A school cafeteria reviews snack stock every Friday and places orders weekly.
